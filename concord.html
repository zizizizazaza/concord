The problem

Policy is enacted once.
Its effects last for years.

Governments make high-stakes decisions — tax, subsidy, visa, tariff — with limited ability to test the outcome first. Spreadsheets miss second-order effects. Consultants take months. And a single AI model that simply hands back an answer can't be audited, challenged, or defended to the public.
